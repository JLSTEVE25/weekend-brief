Curated for the family this weekend
Saturday

🍺 OMB Biergarten — Beat the 90° Heat

It's going to hit 90 tomorrow with basically zero chance of rain — perfect excuse to camp out under the shade at OMB. The boys can run wild on the playground while you and Sara decompress with a Copper or two. Grab pretzels and bratwurst and call it a W.

📍 South Charlotte 💲 $$ 👶 Kids Friendly 🐕 Dog Friendly
Saturday

🐷 The Improper Pig — Pulled Pork Egg Rolls, Trust Us

Plaza Midwood on a hot Saturday? Yes. The Improper Pig has a kids-friendly setup, but the real move is the pulled pork egg rolls and kimchi burger. Unique BBQ-meets-Asian fusion vibe your boys won't expect — but they'll devour the mac & cheese.

📍 Plaza Midwood 💲 $$ 👶 Kids Friendly 🌶️ BBQ/Asian Fusion
Sunday

🥞 300 East — Sunday Brunch in a Victorian Home

Sunday cools to 79° and feels like a reward after Saturday's scorcher. 300 East in Dilworth is family-run, kid-friendly, and the vanilla custard French toast is one of the best brunch dishes in Charlotte. Charming house setting the boys will think is cool.

📍 Dilworth 💲 $$ 👶 Kids Friendly 🥐 Brunch
Family

🏓 Tipsy Pickle — Pickleball + Arcade + Food

Camp North End has Tipsy Pickle, which is basically designed for families like yours. Pickleball courts, golf simulators, arcade games, and decent Tex-Mex. Let the boys burn energy while you enjoy a cold one. Text the Blackburns — Barrett and Shelby would be into this.

📍 Camp North End 💲 $$ 👶 Kids Friendly 🎮 Pickleball + Arcade
Curated for your weekend
Date Night

🔥 Supperland — Saturday Night in a Restored Church

Morgan Wallen is pulling crowds to BofA Stadium, so skip uptown entirely and head to Plaza Midwood. Supperland's wood-fired steakhouse vibe in that gorgeous converted church is still one of Charlotte's best dinner experiences. Hit the speakeasy bar after for nightcaps. 90° during the day means warm patio evenings — perfection.

📍 Plaza Midwood 💲 $$$ 🍷 Speakeasy Bar 🏆 Michelin Cocktails Award
Date Night

🍽️ Rada — Basque Flavors, NY Times-Noticed

If Supperland feels familiar, pivot to Rada in Myers Park. The dover sole is absurd, the wine list is deeply curated, and it's the kind of place that makes you feel like you're somewhere more interesting than a Saturday night. NY Times and Michelin have both taken notice. Reserve now.

📍 Myers Park 💲 $$$ 🏆 NY Times + Michelin 🍷 Wine-forward
Sunday

🥂 The Crunkleton — Cocktails Meet Brunch

Sunday's cooler 79° sets up perfectly for a leisurely brunch at The Crunkleton in Elizabeth. Known primarily as Charlotte's best cocktail bar, their brunch game is sneaky good — Charlotte Hot Brown, biscuits & gravy, and yes, cocktails at 11am are encouraged. Grab a friend and make it a thing.

📍 Elizabeth 💲 $$ 🍸 Cocktail Bar 🥐 Brunch
Date Night

🥃 The Story of Mi Cariño — 200+ Agave Spirits

South End's newest mezcalería opened in January and it's already a scene. Over 200 agave spirits, scratch small plates, and the kind of moody atmosphere that makes a Saturday night feel like an event. Perfect warm-weather date — smoky mezcal, small plates, zero kids. Invite the Blackburns or go solo.

📍 South End 💲 $$ 🥃 Mezcalería ✨ New — Jan 2026
