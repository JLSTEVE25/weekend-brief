🌙 Friday Evening
Supperland Date Night
John's Alumni Weekend stuff will wrap by evening — claim him before Saturday's soccer-party-soccer triple header. Supperland's restored church is still one of the most atmospheric rooms in Charlotte, the wood-fired steaks are dialed, and the speakeasy bar downstairs is perfect for a nightcap. 🌆 Saturday Evening
Rada — You Deserve This After the Morning
Two soccer games plus Oli's birthday at a homestead. By 5 PM you'll have earned a quiet table in Myers Park. Rada's Basque-leaning menu (dover sole, lamb bolognese) has been getting NY Times love for a reason. Ask John to handle bedtime — you've been on duty all morning. Possible drizzle keeps the patio crowd home, so you might even snag a table easily.
Myers Park $$$ Date Night
🌅 Sunday Evening
Mi Cariño — Mezcal & Small Plates in South End
You'll be done teaching HSM by 10:30, Noah's party wraps at 3 — by evening the week is practically over. The Story of Mi Cariño opened in January and the 200+ agave spirit list is calling. Scratch small plates, low lighting, zero kids. It's warm enough (83°) to walk the Rail Trail before or after. Close out the weekend right.
