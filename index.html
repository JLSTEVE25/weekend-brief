Saturday Family
The Improper Pig — BBQ with a Twist
You keep talking about trying this place — here's your excuse. Pulled pork egg rolls, a kimchi burger that somehow works, and a kids menu that won't get side-eyed. Plaza Midwood on a hot Saturday afternoon has energy, and the boys will dig the laid-back vibe. Perfect pre-pool or post-morning-errands lunch.

Sunday Family
300 East — Sunday Brunch in a Victorian Home
Sunday cools down to 75° with possible drizzle — perfect brunch weather. 300 East in Dilworth is family-run, tucked inside a gorgeous Victorian house, and their vanilla custard French toast is the stuff of Charlotte legend. Kids are welcome, portions are generous, and you'll feel like a proper grown-up doing it. Get there early — this place fills.
📍 Dilworth 💰 $$ 👶 Kid-Friendly 🥞 Brunch
Sunday Family
Tipsy Pickle — Pickleball + Arcade + Tex-Mex
If the drizzle pushes you indoors on Sunday, Tipsy Pickle at Camp North End has pickleball courts, golf simulators, and an arcade the boys will not want to leave. The Tex-Mex menu is solid, and they've got margaritas for the parents. Think of it as a one-stop rainy-day win. Invite the Blackburns — Henry and your guys would tear up the arcade.
📍 Camp North End 💰 $$ 👶 Kid-Friendly 🏓 Pickleball
Saturday Date Night
Supperland — Saturday Night in the Church
With Morgan Wallen pulling 70,000 people to Uptown, steer the opposite direction and grab a table at Supperland in Plaza Midwood. The restored church space is stunning at night, the wood-fired steaks are ridiculous, and the speakeasy bar downstairs is worth lingering at. Michelin-recognized cocktails don't hurt either. Book now — the crowd avoiding Uptown will land here.
📍 Plaza Midwood 💰 $$$ 🍸 Speakeasy Bar 🏆 Michelin Cocktails
Saturday Date Night
The Story of Mi Cariño — New Mezcalería in South End
This just opened in January and the buzz is real — 200+ agave spirits, scratch small plates, and a moody, beautiful space in South End. A 90-degree Saturday night practically begs for mezcal cocktails on a patio. This is the kind of spot you go to before everyone else discovers it and the wait becomes unbearable.
📍 South End 💰 $$ 🥃 200+ Agave Spirits ✨ New — Jan 2026
Sunday Date Night
The Crunkleton — Cocktail-Forward Sunday Brunch
Sunday's cooler temps and drizzle make this the ideal morning for The Crunkleton in Elizabeth. Their Charlotte Hot Brown is a cult favorite, the biscuits & gravy are indulgent, and the cocktail program is legitimately one of the best in the city. No kids here — this one's for you two. Sleep in, rally, go slow.
📍 Elizabeth 💰 $$ 🍳 Brunch 🍸 Top Cocktails
Sunday Date Night
Rada — Your Go-To, But Make It Sunday
Sunday evening, rain tapering off, the week looming — treat yourselves to the Dover sole or lamb bolognese at Rada before it all starts again. The NY Times and Michelin have both noticed this place for a reason. That curated wine list pairs perfectly with a "we survived the weekend" mood. You love it here. Go back.
📍 Myers Park 💰 $$$ 🏆 NY Times + Michelin 🍷 Wine List
