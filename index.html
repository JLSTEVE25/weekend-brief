Claude's Picks
Saturday Morning
Strawberry Picking at Carrigan Farms
Peak strawberry season and 78° with full sun — textbook farm morning. U-pick is open through late April. The boys (6 & 4) can pick their own, baby's fine in a carrier. Go early (9–10 AM) before it gets hot. Bring hats and sunscreen.

Invite idea: Grab a family with similar-age kids. Farm mornings are zero-pressure social time.
📍 Mooresville · 30 min 👶 Ages 2+ 🍓 U-pick open ⏰ 9–11 AM
Saturday Lunch
Eddie's Place
10 minutes from Carrigan Farms heading back toward Charlotte. All-day breakfast, cheap, loud enough that nobody cares about your kids. The strawberry-picking-to-Eddie's pipeline is a proven family Saturday.
🍴 Breakfast/American 👶 Kid-friendly 💰 $
Sunday
Splash Pad + Greenway Bike Ride
78° and partly cloudy — ideal splash pad weather without the June heat. Cordelia Park (NoDa): zero-depth splash area, playgrounds, walking trails. The boys ride bikes on the Little Sugar Creek Greenway (flat, paved, wide) — then cool off. Grab lunch at Free Range Brewing across the street: kids' play area, food trucks, beer for you.
💦 Splash pad 🚲 Greenway bikes 🍺 Free Range Brewing 💰 Free/cheap
