🔥 Hot Weekend

Saturday hits 90° — hottest day of 2026 so far

It's going to feel like summer jumped the line. Stay hydrated, lean into shade and patios with misters, and save the outdoor adventures for Sunday when temps drop to a much more comfortable 79°. Cloud cover both days keeps direct sun at bay, but the humidity will be real.

🎵 Tonight

Morgan Wallen — Still the Problem Tour

Saturday at 7 PM, Bank of America Stadium. Uptown will be packed — expect road closures on Mint and Graham by 4 PM. If you're not going, steer clear of the area. If you are, it's going to be a scorcher in those stands — bring water and sunscreen for the standing sections.

Curated for You
Saturday Family

Beat the Heat at Olde Mecklenburg Brewery 🍺

With 90° on tap, OMB's massive shaded biergarten is your best friend. The boys can run wild on the playground while you and Sara grab a Copper or Mecklenburger from the bar. Order the giant soft pretzel and bratwurst plate — easy, no-fuss fuel. Get there by 11:30 before the Morgan Wallen crowd starts migrating. Consider inviting the Blackburns or the Hublers — their kids are the same age and it's an easy yes.

South Charlotte $$ ✓ Kids Friendly Go-To
Saturday Date Night

Supperland — Skip the Stadium, Hit the Speakeasy 🥂

While all of uptown is gridlocked with Morgan Wallen fans, head the opposite direction to Supperland in Plaza Midwood. That restored church dining room is stunning, the wood-fired steaks are incredible, and the speakeasy bar downstairs (Idlewild) is perfect for a nightcap. Book for 8 PM — the Wallen crowd will be locked in at the stadium, so Plaza Midwood will be blissfully chill. This is your kind of night.

Plaza Midwood $$$ Adults Only Go-To
Sunday Family Try New

Sunday Brunch at 300 East 🥞

You've had 300 East on the list — this Sunday is the move. It's a gorgeous Victorian home in Dilworth, family-run, and the vanilla custard French toast is borderline life-changing. The boys will love eating in a "fancy house," and Sunday's 79° with cloud cover makes the walk from parking actually pleasant. The Mims or Fountaine families would be a great invite — Dilworth is right in their zone.

Dilworth $$ ✓ Kids Friendly Want to Try
Sunday Date Night Try New

Lazy Sunday Brunch at The Crunkleton 🍳

If you can get a sitter for a couple hours Sunday morning, The Crunkleton in Elizabeth does a brunch that's basically a cocktail bar pretending to be a restaurant — in the best way. The Charlotte Hot Brown is decadent, biscuits and gravy are legit Southern, and their brunch cocktails are why this place is famous. Grab a patio table, take your time, and pretend you don't have kids for 90 minutes.

Elizabeth $$ Adults Only Want to Try
Saturday Family Try New

Tipsy Pickle — Pickleball + Tacos at Camp North End 🏓

Saturday evening after the heat breaks a little, head to Tipsy Pickle at Camp North End. The boys will lose their minds over the arcade and mini golf sims while you sneak in a pickleball game. The Tex-Mex is solid and the vibe is built for families. It's the opposite side of town from Morgan Wallen chaos — perfect escape. Text Barrett and Shelby Blackburn — this is 100% their speed.

Camp North End $$ ✓ Kids Friendly Want to Try
Saturday Date Night Try New

Tapas & Cava at The Iberian Pig 🐷

South End's buzziest newish spot from the Castellucci Group (the Atlanta crew behind Double Zero). Spanish tapas, killer charcutería boards, and a wine list that leans heavily into Iberian reds and sparkling cava. It's lively without being obnoxious, and the small-plates format makes for a fun, lingering date. Saturday early (6:30) before the Wallen crowd floods uptown bars post-show.

South End $$ Adults Only Want to Try
Sunday Family

Suffolk Punch — Beers + Playground = Win 🛝

Sunday afternoon when it's 79° and overcast is peak Suffolk Punch weather. The patio overlooks the West Plaza playground — the boys play, you drink a local IPA, everyone's happy. It's your go-to for a reason. Low effort, high reward. The Ash family or the Owens are both right around the corner from SouthPark — worth a quick text to see if they want to roll.

SouthPark $$ ✓ Kids Friendly Go-To
